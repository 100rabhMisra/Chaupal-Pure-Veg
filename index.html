About Us

Premier North Indian Dining in Noida Extension

The Best Restaurant in Noida

The Punjab Club - Family Restaurant is your top choice for North Indian cuisine in Delhi/NCR, particularly in Noida. Located in Gaur City, Noida Extension, we offer a refined dining experience with a focus on authentic flavors that will transport you to the heart of Punjab.

Our specialty lies in fragrant biryanis, rich curries, tandoori dishes, artisan pizzas, and delectable desserts. Complement your meal with our selection of craft mocktails and cocktails, carefully curated to enhance your dining experience.

The ambiance at The Punjab Club is elegant with soft lighting, comfortable seating, and sophisticated music - perfect for business lunches, family gatherings, romantic dinners, and celebrations. Experience why we're recognized as one of the best restaurants in Noida.
